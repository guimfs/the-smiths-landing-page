The Smiths

The Queen Is Dead

The newest release of the Manchester's band

The third album will be released in 16th June of 1986. Pre-order your vinyl and wait for the day!

 The album is produced by Morrissey and Marr with Stephen Street and John Porter as engineers. It was recorded at RAK Studios in London and Jacob Studios in Farnham during the winter of 1985.


 'The Boy With The Thorn In His Side', 'Bigmouth Strikes Again' and 'There Is A Light That Never Goes Out' were released as singles.


 The album design were made by Steven Morrissey (sleeve), Steve Wright (photography) and Caryn Gough (layout).
